But why? Why do we need to be able to identify fake news? It is quite simple really. The main reason is because we need to understand when the information provided to us is not real. Fake news is defined as a type of journalism or propaganda that consists of deliberate disinformation or hoaxes spread through traditional print and broadcast news media or online social media. This type of news can create fear, panic, and distrust within people, and if more people get exposed to this information the more fear that will be spread around the world. By being able to identify fake news, you will not only be able to understand what type of news is providing untrustworthy information, but you can also inform others what type of news is fake.
